Inbox Triage Agent

Week 4 M13 · Agentic AI Solo Agent
Claude.ai subscription required

Requires Claude.ai Pro or Team with Cowork access. Open Claude, start a Cowork session, and paste the agent prompt below. No API key or local Python setup needed.

What this does

This agent processes a batch of incoming emails and for each one:

  • Assigns a category: critical support, billing, technical support, partnership, newsletter, or feature request
  • Assigns a priority: urgent (1hr), high (4hrs), medium (24hrs), or low (this week)
  • Drafts a complete, professional reply matching the tone and content of the email
  • Saves all drafts to a JSON file ready for review and send
  • Prints a triage summary table

For support teams or founders managing high email volume, this turns an hour of inbox work into a 2-minute review task.

Subscription required

Claude.ai Pro or Team subscription with Cowork access is required. Cowork lets Claude process your email batch, generate structured output, and save draft files.

Claude.ai Pro Claude.ai Team Cowork feature
Step-by-step in Claude Cowork
Open claude.ai and sign in to your Pro or Team account.
Click "Cowork" in the left sidebar to start a new Cowork session.
Prepare your email batch — paste 3–10 email bodies (or subjects + bodies) into the prompt below.
Paste this prompt into Cowork, replacing the sample emails with your own:
You are an inbox triage agent. I'll give you a batch of emails and you must triage and draft replies for all of them WITHOUT asking me questions. CATEGORIES: critical_support | billing | technical_support | partnership | newsletter | feature_request PRIORITIES: urgent (respond within 1h) | high (4h) | medium (24h) | low (this week) EMAIL BATCH: ---EMAIL 1--- From: sarah.chen@acmecorp.com Subject: Production database down - urgent help needed Body: Our production database has been returning 503 errors for the past 45 minutes. All users are affected. We're losing approximately $2,000 per minute. Need immediate assistance. Our account ID is ACM-28401. ---EMAIL 2--- From: mike@startupxyz.com Subject: Question about API rate limits Body: Hi, we're on the Growth plan and hitting rate limits during peak hours. Wondering if there's a way to get higher limits or if we need to upgrade. What are our options? ---EMAIL 3--- From: partnerships@bigco.com Subject: Co-marketing opportunity Body: We have 50,000 subscribers in your target market. Interested in a joint webinar or newsletter swap? Happy to discuss revenue sharing. For EACH email: 1. Assign category and priority 2. Draft a complete, professional reply (use [Your Name] as signature) 3. Note any action items beyond the reply After processing all emails: - Show a triage summary table: Email | Category | Priority | First line of reply - Save all draft replies as triage-drafts.json Process every email. Do not skip any.
Replace the sample emails with your actual inbox batch before pasting.
Review Claude's draft replies, edit any that need adjusting, and send. The JSON file is downloadable from Cowork.
